Our Services

Bookkeeping

Accurate and timely bookkeeping is the foundation of any successful business. Our bookkeeping services ensure that your financial records are meticulously maintained, giving you a clear and up-to-date picture of your financial health. We handle everything from recording transactions to managing accounts payable and receivable, allowing you to focus on growing your business.

Tax Services

Navigating the complexities of tax regulations can be challenging, but with Titan Financial Services, you’re in capable hands. We offer comprehensive tax services, including corporate (T2) and personal (T1) tax preparation and planning. We also handle the filing of GST/HST, WSIB, and EHT, ensuring compliance with all Canadian tax obligations. In the event of a CRA audit, our team will provide expert support, guiding you through the process with confidence and ease.

Statements & Reports

Clear and accurate financial statements and reports are essential for making informed business decisions. We prepare a variety of financial statements, including income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements, tailored to your specific needs. Our detailed reports offer valuable insights into your financial performance, helping you plan and strategize for the future.

Payroll Services

Managing payroll can be a complex and time-consuming task, but our payroll services are designed to simplify the process. We handle all aspects of payroll management, from calculating wages and deductions to ensuring compliance with Canadian payroll regulations, including PAYE returns. With Titan Financial Services, you can rest assured that your employees will be paid accurately and on time, every time.
